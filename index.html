About Me

My name is Betsy and making beautiful desserts is my passion. It is important to me that my food not only looks breathtakingly beautiful but that it also tastes incredible. This is why I like to experiment with lots of different recipes and modify them in order to find what works out best.

Betsy's Blissful Cakes is a journey and a passion. I began cooking at a very young age, taught by my mother. I started with simple cookies and helping with dinner preparations. I began making dinners in middle school but later discovered that my heart is in desserts. In high school, I decided I wanted to make more than just cookies and bars. I started with decorating cakes and then moved on to desserts such as Crème Brûlée and Napoleons and learned many techniques through videos.

I graduated with my BA in Business and a Concentration in Spanish from the University of Dallas, but in between classes and studying I could be found cooking up desserts in my apartment. I later attended the Culinary Institute of America in Hyde Park, NY for Baking and Pastry Arts and graduated with honors.
